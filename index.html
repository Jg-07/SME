Who are We ??

Society of Mechanical Engineers (SME) is an organization powered by the First-Class Mechanical Engineers in the making, and it is no wonder that the activities conducted by SME are done with perfect vision and synchrony. SME is one of the oldest student run societies in India (Formerly known as Mechanical & Electrical Engineers Association, Established in 1931). The organization focuses on the active development of the students by organizing many informative and technical activities such as guest lectures and workshops by prominent teachers or entrepreneurs. SME also hosts various career guidance programmes to show a clear path for future engineers.

Symposiums
Pinnacle
Pinnacle is a legacy that we have built over the years. Pinnacle is a Mechanical symposium where participants can participate in a wide range of technical and non-technical events. Pinnacle is the SME's magnum opus event that has caught the notice of students, partners and sponsors with our ceaseless effort and matchless creativity. Ascension
Ascension is an intra-college symposium organised by the Society of Mechanical Engineers every year. It is where the freshers are given a warm welcome to the college by entailing them in the various activities conducted by SME.
